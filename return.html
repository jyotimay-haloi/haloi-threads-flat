Returns & Exchanges

Return Window

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items in original, unworn condition with all tags attached.

Conditions
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and unaltered
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Sale items are final sale — no returns or exchanges
  • Custom or made-to-order pieces are non-returnable
How to Return

Email returns@haloithreads.com with your order number and reason for return. We will provide a return label within 24 hours. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving your return.

Exchanges

Size exchanges are subject to stock availability. We recommend placing a new order and returning the original for fastest processing.
